Wildfires in the U.S.A.

Description

This website hosts an up to date collection of interactive maps that show all the current active wildfires in the US as of 12/9/2020. The map can be filtered to show the location of the fires, the weather conditions in the area of the fires and ...... Fire locations are indicated by markers. Click on a marker to zoom into the map and see specific details about the wildfire like size, cause and type.
